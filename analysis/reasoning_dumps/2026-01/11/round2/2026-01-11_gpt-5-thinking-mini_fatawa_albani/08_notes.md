Observed failure:
- "witness that Muhammad is His servant" is missing diacritics.
    - Should have been "witness that Muḥammad is His servant"
- "authentic isnād to the" does not have translation that follows the transliterations.
    - Should have been "authentic isnād (chain of narration) to the"

- "adhere to my sunnah" Sunnah should have been in capitals.

- "Questioner:\nIf the parents" has an unncessary line break. The original Arabic did not have a line break after "السائل:". Likewise after "The Shaykh:"

- "This mushaf which is in the Preserved" is missing diacritics.
    - Should have been: "This muṣḥaf which is in the Preserved"
