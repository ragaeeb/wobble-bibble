Context:
- Book/Author: Fatawa of al-Albānī (Silsilah Huda wa 'l-Nūr)
- Genre add-on used: encyclopedia
- Segment IDs affected: P1a, P2a, P2d, P11, P16, P18

Observed failure:
- "For this reason, the Imam Al-Shāfiʿī" should have been: "For this reason, the Imām al-Shāfiʿī"
    - The "al-" should always be lowercase.
- "Salafī call (Daʿwah Salafiyyah) by" should have been: "Salafī call (Daʿwah Salafīyyah) by"
- "to make Islam a factual reality walking on earth" should be "to make Islām a factual reality walking on earth"
- "Imam Mālik, may Allah have mercy on him, has—meaning—a good and very subtle understanding in his book Al-Muwaṭṭaʾ in the tafsīr"
    - The "al-" should always be in lowercase.
- "obligatory Hajj, or the Hajj of Islam. So if he did not stipulate and was blocked and could not continue the Hajj, upon him in the coming [year] is repetition of the Hajj even if he had performed the obligation of Hajj"
    - Should have been: "obligatory Ḥajj, or the Ḥajj of Islām. So if he did not stipulate and was blocked and could not continue the Ḥajj, upon him in the coming [year] is repetition of the Ḥajj even if he had performed the obligation of Ḥajj"
- "Questioner: If Al-Ḥākim says in his Mustadrak"
    - The "al-" should be in lowercase.
- "permissible for me to perform Umrah twice in one trip" not transliterated correctly.
    - Should have been "permissible for me to perform ʿUmrah twice in one trip".
- "you do not perform Tawaf nor pray" missing diacritics.
    - Should have been: "you do not perform Ṭawāf nor pray"
- "return with a Hajj and an Umrah, and I return" mising diacritics.
    - Should have been: "return with a Ḥajj and an ʿUmrah, and I return"
- "with her to Al-Tanʿīm. So he did" the "al-" should always be lowercase.
    - Should have been: "with her to al-Tanʿīm. So he did"
